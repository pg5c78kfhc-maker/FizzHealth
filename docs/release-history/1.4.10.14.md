# 1.4.10.14 — Nutrient Integrity and Decision Priority

This release establishes a canonical nutrient registry, expands food nutrition inspection/editing to every tracked nutrient, preserves unknown values, adds source/confidence/completeness metadata, refreshes consumed and planned snapshots after explicit food correction, and consolidates time-sensitive urgency ranking and bar colors.

Existing late-day nutrient logic was inspected and preserved. Pace-deficit and guaranteed-visible-without-fixed-rank behavior were added.
