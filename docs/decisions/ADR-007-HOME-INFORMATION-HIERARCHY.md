# ADR-007 — Home information hierarchy

Date: 2026-07-20
Status: Accepted
Related: FH-1265

## Decision

Home renders in this order:

1. Header and date controls.
2. LDL Support, Estimated Maintenance, and Steps decision summary.
3. Ranked nutrient progress bars and What If.
4. Planned and consumed meal sections.
5. All other operational, planning, and intelligence panels.

The remaining lower-page content is not redesigned in this release.
