# Product Owner Acceptance Checklist — FH-1250.2

1. Open **Food → Recommendations**.
2. Confirm recommendations appear as realistic meals or familiar foods for the current meal period.
3. Tap a recommendation and confirm the **Why this meal** explanation opens.
4. Swipe left on a recommendation and confirm it disappears and another candidate takes its place.
5. Confirm the dismissed meal does not immediately return that day.
6. Swipe right on a recommendation and confirm the action sheet opens.
7. Choose **Add to today’s plan**.
8. Return Home and confirm the planned meal appears in the planned-food section and changes projected macro bars/runway.
9. Swipe/remove the planned meal from the Food Log and confirm projected macros recalculate.
10. Return to Recommendations and confirm the removed meal does not immediately reappear that day.
11. Confirm **Eat now** opens the normal quantity-review/logging flow.
12. Confirm **Schedule for later** adds the meal to the chosen date/time.
13. Confirm Home contains only the compact next-meal/day-plan link rather than the large Food Plan card.
