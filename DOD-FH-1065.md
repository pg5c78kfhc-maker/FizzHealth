# FH-1065 — Meal Comparison Engine

## Definition of Done

- Compare two, three, or four hypothetical meals without writing records.
- Evaluate every option against identical current-day totals, plans, targets, activity, and confidence context.
- Rank options using overall decision score with stable LDL, limit-pressure, fiber, and protein tie-breaks.
- Identify the preferred option and explain the most meaningful advantages.
- Show side-by-side decision score, LDL Support, configured-limit pressure, and projected nutrient totals.
- Preserve a complete DecisionTrace with ranking and comparison evidence.
- Automated tests, release verification, and production build pass.
