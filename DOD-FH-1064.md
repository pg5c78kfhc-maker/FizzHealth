# FH-1064 — Decision Simulator Enhancements

## Definition of Done

- The What If simulator supports hypothetical meal additions, meal substitutions, and additional walking.
- Meal simulations accept calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, fiber, saturated fat, sodium, added sugar, and alcohol.
- Substitutions subtract the removed item's nutrition before adding the replacement.
- Results show projected daily totals, target or limit status, LDL Support change, step-likelihood change, and top-priority change.
- The simulator provides up to three specific improvement suggestions.
- Simulations remain in memory and do not write meals, pantry quantities, steps, or health records.
- Automated tests, release verification, and production build pass.
